Frequently Asked

Is it really free?

Yes. The Free tier includes full cross-session semantic memory, multi-model support, the CLI, and 50 saves/month on the Extractor. No credit card needed — ever.

What does BYOK mean?

Bring Your Own Key. You use your own API keys for Claude, OpenAI, Groq, etc. Your data never touches our servers. Memory index runs entirely on your machine.

Can I switch between models mid-session?

Yes. Start with Claude for reasoning, switch to Groq for speed, drop to Ollama for privacy — your memory follows whichever model you use. Seamlessly.

Where is my data stored?

Locally. Your memory index, conversations, and extracted data stay on your machine. Nothing is sent to the cloud unless you explicitly choose to sync.
